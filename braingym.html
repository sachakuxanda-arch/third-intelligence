§ 01 — The Premise

Modern environments heavily train reactive attention, emotional impulsivity, novelty-seeking, and fragmented focus. Very few systems train sustained reasoning, cognitive endurance, emotional regulation under uncertainty, or reflective analysis.

This environment turns thinking itself into a progression system. Not IQ. Not trivia. Not memorisation — but clarity, reasoning stamina, pattern coherence, and perspective shifting. Six trainable attributes that compound over time.

§ 04 — The Loop, Done With You

You don't need to want to exercise your brain to start. Enter a real decision and the system carries the procedure — the framing, the questions, the scheduling, the return. Only two moments stay yours, because they are the training: committing an expectation before the outcome, and honestly judging the gap after. You cannot outsource your iterations — but everything around them, we carry.
