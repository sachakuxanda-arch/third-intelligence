# Predictions Ledger

Registered, measurable, on the record — including the ones that fail.

**Status values:** `OPEN` (registered, not yet resolved) · `SUPPORTED` (evidence meets the stated measure) · `FALSIFIED` (evidence contradicts the stated measure) · `WITHDRAWN` (measure proved unworkable; reasons logged; successor registered under a new ID).

Registered wording is frozen. Edits to claims or measures after registration are not permitted; that is what makes this a ledger rather than a blog.

---

## P-001

**Registered:** 2026
**Claim:** Users who regularly close decision loops improve calibration over time.
**Measure:** Calibration on logged predictions across a ≥90-day window, compared against the user's own baseline period and, where possible, non-practicing controls.
**Operationalization notes:** "Closing a loop" = an expectation recorded before the outcome, plus an outcome honestly logged against it. Calibration scoring method to be fixed before data collection begins (Brier scoring if probabilistic forecasts are captured; otherwise directional hit-rate against stated confidence bands). The scoring method must be committed in this file *before* the first cohort, and may not change mid-measurement.
**Status:** `OPEN`

## P-002

**Registered:** 2026
**Claim:** Systems preserving reasoning outperform systems preserving only outputs.
**Measure:** Decision-revision quality when reasoning context is replayed versus lost — same decision revisited with and without the original reasoning trail, judged on error-correction rate and time-to-revision.
**Operationalization notes:** Requires Counterbalance instrumentation to capture reasoning trails. "Outperform" must be pinned to the two named metrics before testing; no post-hoc metric selection.
**Status:** `OPEN`

## P-003

**Registered:** 2026
**Claim:** Adaptive capacity becomes a more valuable economic asset as intelligence becomes cheaper.
**Measure:** Relative pricing of calibration-linked skills (forecasting, judgment-heavy roles, decision audit) as inference cost falls, tracked against pricing of answer-production-linked skills over the same period.
**Operationalization notes:** The weakest of the three as currently stated — "calibration-linked skills" needs a fixed basket of observable proxies before this can resolve. Candidate proxies to be proposed in the critique log and frozen here once chosen. Until then, this prediction is registered in spirit but not yet resolvable; that limitation is acknowledged rather than hidden.
**Status:** `OPEN`
